REWCI.COM

Registered December 7, 2001 — The Dawn of an Era
REWCI had a great run.
REWCI.com was an early pioneer in online home improvement retail.

Launched in the early days of e-commerce, REWCI helped prove that major home improvement products could be researched, purchased, and delivered online— long before it became standard practice.

As one of the first home improvement retailers on the internet, REWCI built real infrastructure, real fulfillment, and real customer trust. That work earned national recognition, including placement on the Internet Retailer Top 500 Retail Websites.
In 2026, after 25 years we’re calling it quits. No regrets.
